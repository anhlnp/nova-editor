Strategic direction set 2026-06-21: make Nova **Figma/Unity/v0-grade** as a **structured-document editor** (the schema is the "scene file"), NOT v0's generation-first model. Problem solved: good ADRs but no unifying **mental model**; the contracts binding AI↔schema↔canvas↔editor were prose-only conventions → the "many small bugs."

**The model:** `doc/MODEL.md` (v5.6.0) is the **umbrella over the ADRs** — representations & authority (Document authoritative; Live Tree/View/Selection/History/Export are *projections*), two legal edit directions, **I1–I10 invariant catalog** (`packages/editor/src/model/invariants.ts`). Methodology: triage every bug by *which invariant it violates* → fix the **enforcement** (guard/test).
